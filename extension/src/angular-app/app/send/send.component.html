Auto-Fill Your Form

If you have finished filling in your form, you are ready to Auto-Fill college applications with it. Double-check your form (you should also do that after Auto-Fill is done) and go through the following steps.

  1. Select an App to send to. After you select an App, we will download the corresponding template file from our server. That template contains the instructions that AutoFill+ will soon follow to navigate the App website and fill in fields.
  2. Enter your credentials for this App. AutoFill+ needs your credentials to log into the App website. You may also login manually right now to skip this step. More details below.
  3. Click "Send Now" to start sending your form. Enjoy!
